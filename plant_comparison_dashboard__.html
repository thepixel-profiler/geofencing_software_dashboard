Biophilic Active

Live Plants (E.g., Snake/ZZ)

Real biological plants that breathe, grow, and require active watering/soil maintenance.

  • Air Purification: Converts CO2 to oxygen, captures fine dust, removes harmful VOCs inside closed spaces.
  • Low Upfront Cost: High quality live plants with premium pots cost ₹300 - ₹600.
  • Risk of Decay: Overwatering, drafts, or complete lack of sunlight can kill them in weeks.
  • Pest Risk: Soil moisture can breed minor fungus gnats inside closed executive offices.
Est. Upfront (30-40cm): ₹300 - ₹600 Watering: Once in 2-4 Weeks
Premium Choice

Preserved (Non-Dying)

Real natural foliage harvested at peak and chemically stabilized with glycerin/dye.

  • 100% Real Touch: Soft, organic texture and beautiful biological uniqueness. No plastic shine.
  • Zero Upkeep: Never needs water, soil, pruning, or sunlight. Fully dormant forever.
  • High Initial Cost: 30-40cm displays cost anywhere from ₹1,000 to ₹3,500.
  • Water Sensitivity: Glycerin-dyed foliage leaks dye if exposed to high humidity/sprinkling.
Est. Upfront (30-40cm): ₹1,000 - ₹3,500 Watering: NEVER
Permanently Durable

Artificial / Faux

Industrial creations composed of polyurethane, silk, and supportive metal wire structure.

  • Indestructible: Unbothered by freezing AC draft, intense sun, humidity, or complete darkness.
  • Washable: Can be sprayed down with water, dusted vigorously, or bleached without damage.
  • Repetitive Aesthetics: Close inspection reveals fake patterns and plastic shine.
  • Eco-Footprint: Made of non-biodegradable plastics that sit in landfills forever.
Est. Upfront (30-40cm): ₹600 - ₹1,200 Watering: NEVER
